First Post

Published by Hunter Rafuse on 2023-05-19

What is this blog for?

I will write about nothing but hardware and logical approaches to software in regards to hardware.

Background

Growing up I always waited for the next game to come out and I was just amazed at what people could do with some hardware and some software. I saw also some ways where people could be helped in regards to medical devices, self care and tools to help keep them safe. The sky wasn't the limit and as a programmer you could do anything. I played a lot of games and became interested in programming when I wanted to make my own game. I have been programming ever since.

Other languages

I will definitely want some posts to be translated once I get the translation mechanics setup. I am trying to learn Finnish so if anyone who speaks and writes better Finnish than I do, it would be greatly appreciated if you could make a PR and let me know what is missing. I will have a release and a commit for language updates once I get this site going. If there are any other languages that you would like to contribute, just make a PR. Thanks!

On Tutorials

I might do some tutorials or some subtle hints to get you started but mostly it will just be about me sharing my ideas. I will explain the best way that I can about how I approached an issue and what I used to solve it. If you have any suggestions on what you would like to see, I could make a disqus or something for that and other topics. For now I just want to keep it simple. Again, PRs are always welcome.
